Who we are?

SIC is a Mexican company with 25 years supporting the technological markets by providing warranty and out of warranty services for the leading brands in the industry.

In addition we provide custom tailor solutions to our clients offering “Turn key” solutions and delivering other technological solutions that meet customer requirements and when required, integrate and manage the implementation under the “One stop shop” approach.

Additionally we can sale of equipment, IT infrastructure components and accessories.

Our Vision

To be the most recognized company in the American continent, cultivating the confidence of our clients day by day.

Target

Respond to the confidence of the shareholders, guaranteeing the most profitable return of their investments, through the intelligent integration of the professional talents of the organization.
