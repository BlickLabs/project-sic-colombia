Equipment Repair

Diagnostics, Repair and Maintenance of laptops, PCs and Servers.

Diagnostics of Laptops, PCs and Servers

Our team of certified technicians will perform the appropriate diagnose of the equipment utilizing the tools and techniques recommended by the manufacturers and supported by the vast technical experience they posses.
We can provide these services On-site or getting the end-user equipment in out laboratorie.

General Repair

We have a network of local spare parts suppliers that will allow us to repair the equipment.
Having these parts and supported with our certified staff we feel extremely confidence that we can solve the technical issues affecting your equipment.

Maintenance

A physical cleaning of each component is done to avoid overheating and improve the performance of your equipment.

Reinstallation and Configuration of Operating System

If the performance of the equipment starts to decrease our technicians will diagnose the problem and identify the appropriate course of action to solve it, in some cases it would require that the Operating System an drivers need to be "Refreshed" and formatting the HDD also will remove potential Malware already in the system. We will always install the official versions with your manufacturers’ license "Key".

Data Backup
